About Me

I build products that ship, scale, and make business sense.

With a background in Mathematics and Psychology, I'm half data nerd, half "now how does that make you feel". I've lead Product, Data, and Customer teams at a SaaS company on a mission to fix entry-level hiring in South Africa.

I've worn many hats: product owner, data analyst, small business hustler. What ties it all together? A deep love for solving messy problems and turning ideas into real-world impact (like helping more than 60 000 job seekers find employment in South Africa).

I'm obsessed with getting products to market fast, learning from users, and using those insights to drive real results (revenue). Lately, I've been diving headfirst into AI…not just for the hype, but to actually make better product decisions, faster.

I'm a big fan of simple solutions, fast feedback, and building stuff that works.
